Our Mission

We aim to help children engage with their community through volunteering at local hospitals. They assist with non-medical tasks, such as helping in the cafeteria or delivering gifts to patients.

About Us

Our platform connects children aged 7-12 with volunteer opportunities in healthcare settings, helping them grow while making a positive impact.
